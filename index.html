Pre Meet





Come to our pre con meet and greet in Little Tokyo! Browse the many boutiques and restaurants around. The Japanese bookstore Kinokuniya and Daiso are both nearby. If you wish to take public transit, Little Tokyo is accessible by the Gold line. Meet us in Weller Court on September 29 from 12 PM – 4 PM

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Who does the BCLA team consist of and what are their positions?

The Los Angeles team consists of:
Angel Lee who is in charge of applications and vendor relations.
Lesser Tralfamadorian aka Fiddy who is responsible for administration.
Irene Garcia who is in charge of all the wonderful art work.
Joey Gomez who is in charge of donations.

If you have any questions, feel free to send an email to blytheconla@gmail.com or contact us directly on Facebook.



Will BCLA staff be providing lunch?

We will have the food trucks Wicked Fish Tacos and Dina's Dumplings parked in front of the venue. There is also an indoor coffee stand with baked goods, coffee, water and other items of that nature.



What is the price of a regular admission ticket?

$75.00 usd



What is the fee for a vendor table?

$45.00



What is the price of a vendor assistant ticket?

The price for the assistant ticket is $45.00 usd. This however does not include a goodie bag or raffle tickets.



Will there be a special discounted price for children?

Unfortunately not, due to limited space general admission tickets must be purchased at regular price.



When is BlytheCon?

BCLA is taking place September 30th 2018.



What are BCLA hours?

Convention hours are between 10am-5pm.



Will there be parking?

There is a giant lot right in front of the venue and tons of street parking.



When do tickets go on sale?

January 6, 2018



Is there a host hotel?

We were able to secure a block of rooms at The Grafton Hotel on Sunset. The rate is 225 per night (plus tax), which is a fantastic rate when you look around within that area. Unfortunately SPACES ARE LIMITED, the group rate is on a first come, first served basis. The hotel is located right on Sunset Blvd. The Grafton is a very beautiful hotel within a great shopping area, central to a lot of stuff. Please check out the link below to book your rooms. Your card will not be charged at the time of booking, but it will be charged 45 days prior to the reservation. Should you want to cancel your reservation, you must do so before the 45 days which falls on August 17th 2018 (no exceptions).

Grafton Hotel




Should I take public transportation or rent a car?

Honestly, it depends on what your plans are while you’re in Los Angeles. If you plan on doing some sightseeing, I recommend renting a car but there are other options available, like Uber, Lyft, taxis, and the Metro.
