FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Who does the BCLA team consist of and what are their positions?

The Los Angeles team consists of:
Angel Lee who is in charge of applications and vendor relations.
Lesser Tralfamadorian aka Fiddy who is responsible for administration or similar.
Irene Garcia who is in charge of all the wonderful art work.
Joey Gomez who is in charge of donations.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us directly.



How do I know if my vendor application has been received?

If you did not receive a bounceback message, it most likely went through, but if you have doubts please send an email to: Bclavendors@gmail.com and Angel Lee will answer your questions. Please make sure to give us plenty of time to respond as applications are a daunting task.



Will BCLA staff be providing lunch?

We will have various food trucks visiting right outside the lounge area of the building. There is also an indoor coffee stand with baked goods, coffee, water and other items of that nature.



What is the price of a regular admission ticket?

$75.00 usd



What is the fee for a vendor table?

$45.00



What is the price of a vendor assistant ticket?

The price for the assistant ticket is $45.00 usd. This however does not include a goodie bag or raffle tickets.



Will there be a special discounted price for children?

Unfortunately not, due to limited space general admission tickets must be purchased at regular price.



When is BlytheCon?

BCLA is taking place September 30th 2018



What are BCLA hours?

Usually Convention hours are between 10am-6pm



Where is BCLA taking place?

The Reef, 1933 S Broadway, Los Angeles, CA 90007



Will there be parking?

There is a giant lot right in front of the venue and tons of street parking.



When do tickets go on sale?

Right now, we are planning for tickets to go on sale either in Dec. or Jan.



When will vendor applications go up?

Vendor applications are up now and will be open till Dec. 1st. The vendor list will become public on Dec. 15th.



Will discount tickets be available for vendor assistants?

If you have an assistant helping you vend discount tickets will be available however helpers will not be eligible for a goodie bag.



Would you recommend taking public transportation or renting a car?

Honestly it all depends on what your plans are while you’re in Los Angeles if you plan on doing some sightseeing I recommend renting a car but there are other options to you like Uber, Lyft, taxi’s, the Metro and public buses.



Who can I contact about Donations?

Joey Gomez oversees donations please contact him directly if you are interested in donating for BCLA. Donations are due by August 2018.
